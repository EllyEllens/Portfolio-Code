Sranan Techies
(Creative Tech Hub Caribbean)

As team captain of Sranan Techies (Creative Tech Hub Caribbean), my team and I represent Suriname in competitions. Our team develops innovative applications. Our interest in participating in competitions is driven by our desire to apply and further develop our skills and qualities. Additionally, we see this opportunity as a way to contribute to the movement towards equal representation of women in the IT sector.
