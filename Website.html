About "HER"

Driven by an early passion and desire to advance women's representation in the IT sector, she strives to support and justify this movement. By getting the opportunity to work with her team members at Sranan Techies (Creative Tech Hub Caribbean), Ielaiza aims to inspire women and demonstrate that the IT sector is accessible to both men and women.
